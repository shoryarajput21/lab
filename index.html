Labotronics Scientific is a leading US-based manufacturer of high-quality laboratory equipment, designed to support scientists, researchers, educational institutes,
and innovators in advanding scientific research.

We offer a wide range of lab equipment, including an Autoclave, Centrifuge, Incubator, Freezer, Gradient PCR Machine, PCR Cabinet, Laboratory Balance, ELISA Reader, Drying Cabinet, Fume Hood. Glassware Washer, Hematology Analyzer, Ice Maker Machine, Cell Counter, Furnace, Blood Bank Refrigerator, Rotary Evaporator, Oven, Spectrophotometer, Test Chamber and more.

Our commitment is to deliver products that prioritize safety, reliability, and exceptional quality.

We take pride in establishing lasting relationships with our customers and guarantee the quality and durability of our entire equipment range. Each product undergoes thorough individual inspection by our skilled technicians before being dispatched.

We are certified by ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 13485:2015, demonstrating our commitment to quality and operational excellence. Our calibration laboratory is compliant with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 standards. Additionally, our products meet international standards such as the 2014/35/EU Low Voltage Directive and the 2014/32/EU Measuring Instruments Directive.
We can also provide IQ, OQ, and PQ Certifications with our products. For more details, please contact us.
