About Us

Labotronics Scientific is a top-leading manufacturer of laboratory equipment, providing customized solutions and comprehensive support to industries such as Life Sciences, Biotech, Research Institutes, and the Chemical and Pharmaceutical sectors.

We aim to promote scientific and technological progress by consistently broadening our product line. Our commitment to building solid and enduring business relationships with each of our valued customers.

At Labotronics Scientific, we prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to deliver exceptional service and support. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping our customers achieve their scientific goals by providing customized solutions.

We provide a broad range of laboratory Equipment, including Laboratory Incubators, Balances, Air Sampler, Chillers, Gas Detectors, Ovens, Test Chambers, Autoclaves, pH Meters, Freezers, Blood bank refrigerator, Furnaces, Sterilizers, UV Analyzers, Homogenizers, Microscope, Oil Particle Counter, Real Time PCR, Water Testing Instrument and many more.

We are certified by ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 13485:2015 standards, demonstrating our dedication to quality and operational excellence. Our calibration laboratory complies with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 standards.

We also offer IQ, OQ, and PQ certifications for our products.

"We encourage our clients' success by focusing on innovation, cGMP production, and a wide range of customized services."
